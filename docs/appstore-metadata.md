## Description
Aether turns every link you save into structured knowledge.

Drop any URL — YouTube videos, Instagram posts, articles, research papers, blog posts — and AI automatically extracts, transcribes, analyzes, and summarizes the content into your personal searchable vault.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Share a link from any app (YouTube, Instagram, Safari, Chrome)
2. AI extracts content — transcribes videos, OCRs images, scrapes articles
3. Get rich AI-generated summaries with key insights and entities
4. Search your entire vault by meaning, not just keywords

KEY FEATURES:
• Instant Capture — Share from any app via iOS Share Extension
• YouTube Transcription — Gemini AI transcribes video audio
• Instagram Vision — AI analyzes carousel images with OCR
• AI Summaries — Claude generates structured insights
• Semantic Search — Find anything by meaning using vector embeddings
• Auto-Labels — Content auto-organized by source (YouTube, Instagram, GitHub, etc.)
• Bilingual — Full English and Turkish language support
• Dark Mode — Beautiful obsidian dark interface

SUPPORTED SOURCES:
YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, Reddit, GitHub, Medium, Wikipedia, arXiv, Stack Overflow, Hacker News, and any web page.

Your knowledge, always searchable, always yours.
