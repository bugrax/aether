FAQ

How do I save a link?
Use the Chrome extension, iOS Share Extension, or paste a URL directly in the app.

How long does processing take?
Most URLs are processed within 30 seconds. YouTube videos may take up to 60 seconds.

What sources are supported?
YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, Reddit, GitHub, Medium, Wikipedia, arXiv, and any web page.

Is my data private?
Yes. All data is associated with your account only. See our Privacy Policy.
